About Lumera

01. Studio Profile

Lumera Studio is an elite creative visual production house working at the intersection of wedding cinema and fine-art events photography. Based in Luxembourg and collaborating globally, we partner with couples and corporate hosts to construct striking visual memories.

Our core philosophy is simple: we don't just record events; we document their soul. We bring cinematic lighting, framing, and emotional weights to every frame we capture.

02. Core Services

  • Wedding Cinematic Short Films
  • Corporate Galas & Networking Coverage
  • Fine Art Anniversary Portrait Sessions
  • Full pre-shoot creative art direction
  • Professional editing & color grading
  • Widescreen drone shots (Licenced operators)
